About the company

Kaz-Travertine LLP

Kaz-Travertine LLP is a Central Asian manufacturing company specializing in the extraction and processing of natural travertine. Combining the region's natural resources, modern technology, and professional expertise, the company produces high-quality natural stone products for architecture and construction.


The company is focused on developing Kazakhstan's export potential and promoting the region's natural stone on the international market.

Infrastructure

Own Quarry

Kaz-Travertine is developing a natural travertine deposit in Kazakhstan. Mining is taking place at the Degeres-1 and Degeres-3 sites. The deposit's total reserves amount to over 1,300,000 m³ of natural stone, ensuring stable long-term supplies.

Infrastructure

Own Factory

Kaz-Travertine processes natural travertine using modern stone processing equipment. Our production facilities enable us to manufacture blocks, slabs, plates, and facing tiles in various formats for architectural and construction projects.

Quality assurance

Certified & Tested

🌿 Natural & Environmentally Safe Stone
❄️ High Frost Resistance — Certified
🏗️ Laboratory Tested Physical Properties

Kaz-Travertine's natural travertine has undergone laboratory testing, confirming its excellent physical and mechanical properties, environmental friendliness, and biological neutrality.


The stone is safe for use in construction and architecture and is highly frost-resistant, successfully withstanding freeze-thaw cycles.


Further confirmation of quality is our collaboration with Kamen Stroy Expert LLC (USSA-gran) — a Russian stone processing company specializing in natural stone and complex architectural projects.
