About Kaz-Travertine LLP

Kaz-Travertine LLP is a Central Asian manufacturing company specializing in the extraction and processing of natural travertine. Combining the region's natural resources, modern technology, and professional expertise, the company produces high-quality natural stone products for architecture and construction.

Kaz-Travertine natural stone is distinguished by its strength, natural expressiveness, and durability, making it widely used in building cladding, architectural elements, and design projects.

The company is focused on developing Kazakhstan's export potential and promoting the region's natural stone on the international market.

Own quarry

Kaz-Travertine is developing a natural travertine deposit in Kazakhstan. Mining is taking place at the Degeres-1 and Degeres-3 sites.
The deposit's total reserves amount to over 1,300,000 m³ of natural stone, ensuring stable long-term supplies for construction and architectural projects.

Recognition from industry professionals

Kaz-Travertine's natural travertine has undergone laboratory testing, confirming its excellent physical and mechanical properties, environmental friendliness, and biological neutrality. The stone is safe for use in construction and architecture and is highly frost-resistant, successfully withstanding freeze-thaw cycles.

Further confirmation of the quality of our stone is our collaboration with the Russian stone processing company Kamen Stroy Expert LLC (USSA-gran), a manufacturing company specializing in the processing of natural stone and the implementation of architectural projects of varying complexity.

The company's specialists, who have extensive experience working with natural stone, highly praised Kaz-Travertine travertine, noting its distinctive natural structure, stable physical and mechanical properties, and suitability for use in architectural and construction projects.

Natural & Environmentally Safe Stone

Travertine is a natural stone formed through natural geological processes. The material is environmentally friendly, biologically neutral, and safe for humans.

The stone does not emit harmful substances and does not promote the growth of bacteria and fungi, and its porous structure ensures natural micro-ventilation of the surface and helps maintain a favorable microclimate in the premises.

Its environmental safety and durability make travertine a sought-after material in modern architecture and construction.
